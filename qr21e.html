He would also carry goods to Nant Gwrtheyrn. Later, Mrs Dinah Owen kept an eye on things for several years. The last family to run a shop here were the Jordans. The lads in the village used to socialise and gather here. After closing as a shop, the building was bought by Ymddiriedolaeth Nant Gwrtheyrn as an office.
