Capel Moreia – Independent Chapel

On 29 August 1880, a meeting was held in the barn of Llithfaen Uchaf to discuss establishing an Independent movement in Llithfaen. The land was bought for £61.14.0. The chapel was built for £189, seating 300 people. After building the first chapel, a gale ripped the roof off, which was found whole in a nearby field. A preaching meeting was held in November 1881 to establish and officially open the chapel, naming it Moreia. The chapel closed on 25 March 1997.
