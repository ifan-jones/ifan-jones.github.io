Haircutting Shack

Wil Bryncelyn’s haircutting business was in a shack under a big tree opposite Matt H Williams’ garage (Delfryn). He also went around giving haircuts. It was a wooden shack, between two dilapidated buildings by Isallt, Isgoed and the garage.

Dewi Williams, Brynmor remembers that Wil Bryncelyn’s haircutting shack was located under a big tree opposite Matt H Williams’ garage. There was a wooden shack between two dilapidated buildings by Isallt, Isgoed and the garage. Glyn Roberts, Llithfaen Isaf remembers that Wil Bryncelyn came to cut his grandfather’s hair in the cowshed.
