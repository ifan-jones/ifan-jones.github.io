Carnguwch Mill

The single storey mill has been altered, probably in conjunction with the demolition of the millerâ€™s cottage to which it was attached. Access is by a wide door on the west side, and there are no windows. The empty wheel pit survives at the north-east gable, with a blocked hole for the axle. There is now a pond below the mill, but no trace of the leat which brought water to the original wheel.
